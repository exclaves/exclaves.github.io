Lloyd's relaxation with color coded cells

March 31, 2018

I saw an interesting visualization on Jason Davie’s website last night of Lloyd’s relaxation algorithm. I thought it was fun to watch the cells slowly change number of sides and irregularity, so I extended his visualization to color cells differently depending on their irregularity. Source is, well, in the source.
