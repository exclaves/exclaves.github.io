Signal Culture: Realtime tools for 3D video slicing

April 17, 2018

At Signal Culture, I’m working on a generalization of the technique I used to make my “isometric spacelapses.” It’s a realtime application for interactively slicing and sliding a video as a 3-dimensional object. A release version and source code will be out by the time I finish my residency.
